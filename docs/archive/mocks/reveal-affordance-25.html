THE REVEAL AFFORDANCE — 25 WAYS IN

Same W17 card in all twenty-five — only the "this opens" cue changes. You killed the oval, then the box; fair. A—E are container-free: words, arrows, the seam itself, the card's own edges. F is "if it must be a shape, these aren't buttons from a template." Everything ships with the tap target being the whole card, so the cue only has to SAY it, not BE the hitbox.

MY READ

R10 (Footer Rail) is my pick — it's unmissable without being a floating widget, because it's the card's own bottom edge doing the work; nothing else in the app looks like it, so it becomes THE "sealed pick" signature. R9 (Light in the Crack) is the most elegant — the W17 seam earning its keep — best paired with R1's plain words so the cue isn't motion-only. R2/R5 are the strongest pure-typography routes if you want zero added objects. R24 has the most personality; R21 the most restraint. Tap target is the full card in every case — this choice is purely about what SAYS "open me."
