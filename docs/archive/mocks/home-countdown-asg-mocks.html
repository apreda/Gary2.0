C · THE LIVE DECK

A scrollable row instead of one big panel

Rather than picking one "next" game to feature, this swaps the single panel for a horizontally-scrolling deck of mini-cards — WC semi, All-Star Game, MLB's return — each with its own small countdown or a LIVE tag. Less spotlight on one game, more of a "here's the whole week at a glance" scan.

Why it wins
  • Scales naturally — a slow week shows 2 cards, a loaded week shows 6, nothing gets cramped into one panel.
  • Live games and countdowns share the same visual language, so "happening now" vs "happening soon" reads instantly.
Watch out: no single moment gets the spotlight — if there's one marquee game you want Home to sell hardest, this direction undersells it next to a swipeable row.
