## Strategy Pillars

I'll mix content types because the X algorithm rewards variety, and a single account that only posts pick tweets gets capped fast. The ratio I'm targeting:

1. **Daily Pick Thread (1/day)** — Hook tweet → reasoning reply → app CTA reply. Highest engagement-per-post, each tweet in a thread counts impressions independently. This is the workhorse.
2. **Recap Tweets (1/morning)** — Yesterday's W/L. Owns wins AND losses in plain language. Builds credibility (people screenshot recap tweets when picks hit). High virality potential when on a heater.
3. **Reply Engagement Runs (5–10/day)** — Top-tab tweets with >200 views, betting community accounts. Spaced 5–10 min. Drives profile clicks → followers → future organic impressions.
4. **Reaction Posts (when news breaks)** — Player IL, line move, big game story. Gary's quick angle. These ride trending news for free reach.
5. **Slate Reads (1–2/week)** — Saturday or Sunday morning "here's what's interesting today across the slate" — broader than one pick, gets shared as a reference.

What I'm NOT doing: image-only posts (they tank reach 10–20x per existing data), generic AI slop, salesy CTAs, hashtag spam.

## Reply Engagement — Operating Rules (from existing playbook)

- Replies 5–10 min apart
- Target tweets >200 views (Top tab, not Latest)
- Short, human, non-salesy. Vary phrasing — never repeat
