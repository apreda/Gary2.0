# Tier 3 — The Creator Funnel (the Cal AI engine)

> The machine that actually converts installs: real humans posting native-style videos using the app.
> Their voice, their style, our product moment. Volume over polish. This doc is the operating playbook.

## The funnel math (Cal AI's observed rates)
- Message ~30 creators/week → expect ~10% replies → ~2-4 posts/week at steady state.
- Pay $50-100 per post for micro creators (10K-150K). Never negotiate hard at this tier; speed beats price.
- Scale rule: after 10 posts, compare installs-per-creator-niche (App Store Connect Campaigns, `cr_<handle>`
  tokens) and double down on the niche that converts. Kill niches that don't after 2 tests.

## Targeting criteria (all must hold)
1. 10K-150K followers on TikTok or IG Reels. Below 10K = no reach; above 150K = wrong price tier.
2. REAL comments (actual sentences from actual humans, not emoji walls or bot spam).
3. Niche: sports betting, degen/sweat content, fantasy sports, sports comedy, "picks" reactors, bankroll
   vloggers. US-based audience.
4. Posts consistently (3+/week) and has at least one video in the last 30 days that clearly outperformed
   their median (shows the algorithm still tests their content).
5. No fake-cash-flash accounts, no "guaranteed locks" sellers — their audience is trained wrong and their
   association damages the brand.

## The offer (DM templates — founder sends from a Gary-adjacent or personal account)

**First touch (short, human, no corporate speak):**
> yo — love the [specific video reference]. I run Gary, an AI handicapper app that posts its picks before
> games and grades every one public, wins and losses. Want to do a paid post showing it however you'd
> naturally use it? $[75] for one video, no script, you keep full creative control. Only rule is honesty:
> show the losses tab too if you want, it's kind of the whole point.

**Follow-up (3 days, once):**
> bumping this once — happy to send you the app free either way, the graded tape speaks for itself.

**Terms message (after a yes):**
> perfect. deal: one TikTok/Reel, your style, minimum 20s, live for at least 30 days, #ad disclosed.
> put your link (betwithgary.ai/c/YOURHANDLE) in your bio for the week or pin it in comments.
> I pay $[75] via [Venmo/PayPal] within 24h of the post going live. Draft not required, but if you send
> one I'll turn feedback around same day.

## The creative brief (what they get after agreeing)
- **The wow moment to show on screen:** the graded tape — every pick from yesterday marked won or lost,
  and the season P/L at $100 flat. Let them react to it however they want ("this AI just posts its record??").
- Secondary moments: today's card revealing; the pick card share; Gary's X verdicts.
- **Their voice, never our script.** Suggested angles, not lines: "I tailed an AI for a week", "this AI
  grades itself and I can't stop watching", "the only picks account that shows its losses".
- **Hard rules (non-negotiable, FTC + brand):** #ad or Paid Partnership label ON. Never say "guaranteed",
  "lock", "free money", or claim specific profit promises. Never fake a betslip win. 21+ audience framing.
- Their tracked link: `betwithgary.ai/c/<their handle>` (bio for a week, or pinned comment).

## Tracking & payout
- Every creator gets `betwithgary.ai/c/<handle>` → App Store with `ct=cr_<handle>`. Clicks land in
  `link_clicks` same-day; installs appear in App Store Connect → Campaigns once the token clears ~5 installs.
- Weekly rollup (part of the Monday scoreboard): clicks + installs per creator, cost per install.
- Pay within 24h of the post going live, always. Fast payment is the whole reputation in this niche.

## Ops cadence
- **Weekly:** I produce a vetted candidate list (15-20 handles w/ notes) → founder DMs 20-30 → track in
  `GaryMarketing/creator-lists/` (one dated file per week, columns: handle, niche, followers, status, price, link).
- **Budget:** $200-400/mo from the envelope (2-5 posts) until a niche proves CPI, then rebalance from ASA.
