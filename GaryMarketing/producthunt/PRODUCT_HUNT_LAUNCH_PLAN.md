### Full Description
Gary analyzes real-time sportsbook odds, historical stats, injuries, matchup data, revenge factors, and narratives using GPT-5.1 and Perplexity AI to generate confidence-rated picks for every game on the board.

**What makes Gary different:**

- **Every game, every day** — not just "best bets." Gary covers the full slate across 10+ leagues including NBA, NFL, NCAAB, NCAAF, MLB, NHL, EPL, and WBC
- **100% free** — no subscriptions, no paywalls, no premium tiers. Every pick is free, always
- **Transparent analysis** — see exactly why Gary likes each pick: key factors, matchup context, trend data, and confidence level
- **Player props** — smart prop suggestions based on recent performance and matchup dynamics
- **Billfold tracking** — monitor Gary's pick history and performance over time
- **Daily Pick of the Day (POTD)** — Gary's highest-confidence pick, highlighted daily

Gary is for informational and entertainment purposes only. We don't facilitate gambling, accept deposits, or place bets.
